- **Color 1 (Upper body, top of legs)**
  - Cut **8 Part 1**

- **Color 2 (Belly, bottom of legs)**
  - Cut **8 Part 2**
  - **squid only**
    - Cut **2 Part 5**
- **Octopus & Squid (eyes)**
  - Cut **2 Part 3 (white)**
  - Cut **2 Part 6 (black)**
